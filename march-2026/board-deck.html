Why We're Missing Targets
Resource Imbalance
10x billing SAs/PMs vs 4 PSA - project load per PSA SA was 2.3x higher than billing; team was structurally set up to fail in PSA targets
Process Undefined
No documented, client-signed onboarding plan - SAs operating without standard session structure, scope guardrails or graduation criteria; "we're kind of making it up as we go"
Sales Handoff Quality
Deals arriving without required data assets (API access, exports, product catalog); SA time consumed sourcing what should arrive with the deal; no gate enforced at intake
Product / Performance
Integration blockers (QuickBooks, ConnectWise, etc.) slowed final QA and completion - confirmed repeatedly in the workshop
Recovery Plan
✓Team Structure - Unified SA team (billing + PSA blended) + PM cross-training boot camp

✓Sales Handoff - Standardized brief at deal close, enforced data requirements, no onboarding clock until qualifying assets received
