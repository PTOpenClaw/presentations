Outlook & Commentary

Forward-Looking Notes | April 2026 & Beyond
REVENUE OUTLOOK — APRIL
▸ Billing: Net -$6K (March Odin adjustments roll off); Billing-only +$11K.

▸ PSA Legacy: -$12K (continued churn/credits); PSA Web: +$13K (ramping).

▸ Payments: normalized after ACH catch-up residuals — expect ~$170–180K/month.

▸ Total MRR expected ~$3.0M range in April.
EBITDA & CASH
▸ EBITDA: $679K (20.7%) in March, +$76K vs. budget; YTD $2.16M (22.3%), +$459K vs. budget.

▸ MoM down from 24.5% — incentive accrual resumed ($178K) after zero Jan/Feb.

▸ Watch: if revenue shortfalls widen and people savings narrow as hiring ramps, the cushion compresses quickly.

▸ Cash: $3.73M at March 31 (-$427K MoM). April: ~$2.9M after Q1 interest; recovery to ~$3.3M by end of May.
COST WATCH ITEMS
▸ Hosting (Vervint): $401K actual, +$67K over budget; run rate ~$74K/month over. Needs budget revision or optimization plan.

▸ R&D Contractors: Softserve $42K in March vs. ~$10K expected. Open action item with Ray.

▸ Technology Spend: +$31K over budget; root cause: AI +$13K, UKG accrual miss $13K, HubSpot $2.5K. Confirm recurring vs. one-time.

▸ HC 18 under budget — FTE savings (+$808K YTD) absorbing overruns. Monitor as hiring ramps.
RETENTION HIGHLIGHTS
▸ Billing net retention 101.4% MoM — expansion ($77K upsell) exceeded contraction. Strongest month in trailing 5.

▸ PSA Web MRR +18.5% MoM ($26.7K → $31.7K); net retention 115.3% — ramping but needs to accelerate.

▸ Payments MRR +36% MoM ($158K → $215K) — TRX ACH migration ramp + Paya past-due residuals. Normalizing April.

▸ ARR growth decelerated to 2.7% TTM (from 6.2% prior year) — new deal sizes declining in Billing and PSA.
